About

Hello! Welcome to my portfolio, My name is Parth Arora, I am founder/owner of Heiko Photography. I am an Ottawa based photographer who specializes in portraits and event photography.

I have worked with multiple organizations over the time and built up this portfolio, I am available for hire for photoshoots of all sorts. My portfolio includes 3 major types of photography, portrait photography, product photography, and random photography.

I am available for hire at very affordable prices, I hope you enjoy my work. If interested, please reach out to me via email or my social media handles to book a session.
